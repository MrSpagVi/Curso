Part I 
  
The texts below are points of reference that enable political theorists of many kinds to take part in a 
common conversation. Everyone studying political theory at the graduate level should aspire to be 
familiar with them. 
 
Students must demonstrate a good understanding of the authors and texts followed by (R) to pass 
the department’s general exam in political theory.
